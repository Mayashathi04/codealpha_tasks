7 Wonders of the World

MODERN

Great Wall of China

Built over centuries to protect Chinese states against invasions, it stretches over 13,000 miles and is a masterpiece of defensive architecture.

ANCIENT

Petra

An ancient city in Jordan carved directly into vibrant red sandstone cliffs, it served as a major trading hub for the Nabataean Empire.

MODERN

Colosseum

Located in Rome, this massive stone amphitheater was opened in 80 AD and hosted gladiatorial contests for audiences of over 50,000 people.

MODERN

Chichen Itza

A powerful Maya city in Mexico, it features the iconic El Castillo pyramid designed to reflect the Maya calendar and astronomical alignments.

MODERN

Machu Picchu

Perched high in the Andes Mountains of Peru, this 15th-century Inca citadel was built with dry-stone walls and remained hidden for centuries.

MODERN

Taj Mahal

This stunning white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, was built by Emperor Shah Jahan as a symbol of eternal love for his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

MODERN

Christ the Redeemer

Standing 98 feet tall atop Mount Corcovado in Brazil, this Art Deco statue overlooks Rio de Janeiro as a symbol of peace and welcoming.
